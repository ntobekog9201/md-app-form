TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Any person with a South African Identity can join who is 17-99 years  (under 18 years should bring consent from the guardian. If no guardian is available, an affidavit). 2. WAITING PERIOD

1.Clients joining from another Funeral provider must provide proof of being in good -standing as at time of joining. This will evidenced by production of a valid policy document and verifiable 3 months payment history. The waiting period will subsequently be 1 month. 2.18 months waiting period for suicide cases. 3. 0ne-month waiting period in case of accidental death. 4.6 Months waiting period in the case of natural death. 5. Child is covered under the parent until he/she turns 21 years, full-time students are covered under parents until he/ she turns 25 years. 6.Pregnancy should be reported within 3 months .
